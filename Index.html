🎯 Problem Statement

Many students have old books that they want to sell, but they don’t know whom to sell to. Because of this, they often give books to scrap dealers (“raddi”) at very low prices or throw them away.

💡 Our Solution

We created BookLoop, a free online platform where anyone can buy and sell old books easily. Just upload your book’s photo, name, subject, standard, and address — buyers can directly contact you!

👥 Target Users

🎓 School Students (8th–12th)

🏫 College Students

📖 Book Lovers looking for affordable study material

⚙️ Technologies Used

✨ HTML, CSS, JavaScript

🔥 Firebase Authentication (Login System)

☁️ Firestore Database (Book Storage)

🖼️ Firebase Storage (Image Uploads)

🚀 Firebase Hosting (Free Deployment)

🧩 System Architecture

Frontend (HTML, CSS, JS) ↔ Firebase Auth ↔ Firestore ↔ Storage

All integrated on Firebase — no paid servers required!

🌟 Features

✅ Free Login & Signup

✅ Add Book (photo, name, subject, class, address)

✅ Search & Filter Books

✅ Contact Seller Directly

✅ Admin Dashboard

✅ Mobile Friendly Interface

🚀 How It Works

1️⃣ User Creates Account

2️⃣ Adds Book Details + Photo

3️⃣ Buyers Browse Books

4️⃣ Contact Seller & Finalize Deal

Simple, Fast & Free!
